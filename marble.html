Marble Queen

Marble Queen Pothos will thrive in bright, indirect sunlight. It can also survive lower light conditions, however the white colour in the leaves can be lost and revert to green if the light is too low. Avoid direct sunlight as this can burn the leaves. Pothos like their soil kept on the drier side. Water well and allow the top 50% of the soil to dry out before watering again. In the winter, allow the soil to completely dry between watering.
