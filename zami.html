Zamioculcas

The Zamioculcas zamiifolia, or ZZ Plant, is a tropical perennial native to Eastern Africa. Though it can tolerate lower indirect light, the ZZ thrives in medium to bright indirect light. It will be happy with a minimum of six hours per day and can tolerate up to 12 during the warmer months.
